--- layout: base_slides logo: "GTN" title: Analysis workflows contributors: - chrisbarnettster - tsenapathi --- # What is MD analysis? --- ![](images/platform_overview.png) - Using outputs from MD simulations to look for trends in physical and chemical behaviour. - Timeseries analysis, distribution and correlation analysis are popular. --- # Tools - VMD (builtin plugins and custom scripts) - MDAnalysis - MDTraj - Bio3D .footnote[ [a more extensive list of viewers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_molecular_graphics_systems)] --- # Tools that are in BRIDGE - Bio3d suite (PCA etc.) - MDAnalysis suite (timeseries etc.) - MDTraj --- # Standard structural analysis ![](images/platform_overview.png) --- # RMSD - Root Mean Square Deviation .pull-left[ ![Snapshot of RMSD plot](images/RMSD_plot_33.png "RMSD plot for a short CBH1 simulation") - Root mean square deviation of atomic positions. ] .pull-right[ ![Snapshot of RMSD histogram](images/RMSD_Histogram_Plot_34.png "RMSD histogram for a short CBH1 simulation") - A measure of quantitative similarity. ] --- --- # RMSF - Root Mean Square Fluctuation .pull-left[ - A measure of conformational variance. Can be linked to spectroscopic outcomes (experimental). ] .pull-right[ ![Snapshot of RMSF plot](images/rmsf_plot_23.png "RMSF plot for a short CBH1 simulation") ] .footnote[http://thegrantlab.org/bio3d/html/rmsf.html] ??? $$ f_i=\sqrt{(1/(M-1))\sum_{j=1}^{n} (\vert\vert r_i^{j}-r_i^i{0} \vert\vert ^{2})} $$ , where f_i is the RMSF value for the ith atom, M the total number of frames (total number of rows of xyz), r_i^j the positional vector of the ith atom in the jth frame, and r_i^0 the mean position of ith atom. ||r|| denotes the Euclidean norm of the vector r. --- ## PCA - Principal Component Analysis .pull-left[ - Convert from a set of correlated observations (movement of all atoms in protein) to a set of principal components which are linearly independent (or uncorrelated). - The first principal component has the highest variance and so on. - Useful to find important modes of the protein. ] .pull-right[ ![Snapshot of PCA plot](images/PCA_plot_46.png "PCA plot for a short CBH1 simulation") ]
